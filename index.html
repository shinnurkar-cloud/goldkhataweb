About GoldKhata

GoldKhata is a production-grade SaaS platform designed for lenders, jewellers and pawn-broking businesses to manage gold, silver and personal loans digitally.

  • Customer onboarding
  • Loan account management
  • Pawn ledger management
  • Automatic interest calculation
  • Repayment tracking
  • Settlement management
  • Invoice and receipt generation
  • Business reporting and analytics

GoldKhata operates as a business management SaaS platform and does not directly issue loans.
